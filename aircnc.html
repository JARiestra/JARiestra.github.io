About this Dog

"Hi, there! My name is Dug. I have just met you, and I love you!"


Originating in China, pugs were prized by the Emperors of China and lived in luxurious accommodations, sometimes even being guarded by soldiers. Pugs were bred to be companions, and that's exactly what they do best. They crave attention and are very lovable. Pugs are playful dogs, which is why Dug's favorite activity is going to the dog park. Pugs have a life span of 12 to 15 years. On average, pugs weigh 14 to 18 pounds and grow up to 14 inches tall. We adopted Dug from an animal shelter. The training was a little challenging because pugs tend to be stubborn and difficult to housebreak. Now, Dug is an obedient pug with an affectionate and charming personality.
